About Garden Developer

Passionate about bringing nature into your life through premium plants, expert guidance, and exceptional service since 2009.

Our Story

Founded in 2009 by horticulturist Sarah Johnson, Garden Developer began as a small local nursery with a simple mission: to make high-quality plants and expert gardening knowledge accessible to everyone.

What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted partner for thousands of garden enthusiasts, from beginners taking their first steps into plant parenthood to experienced gardeners seeking rare and exotic specimens.

Today, we're proud to be a leading provider of premium plants, offering everything from hardy outdoor varieties to delicate indoor companions, along with the expertise and support to help them thrive.

  • 15+ years of expertise
  • 5000+ happy customers
  • 1200+ plant varieties
