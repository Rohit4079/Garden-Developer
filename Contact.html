Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does a garden design project take?

Most design projects take 2-4 weeks from consultation to final plans, depending on complexity and size.

Do you provide plant guarantees?

Yes! We offer a 30-day guarantee on all plants and a 1-year warranty on professional installations.

Can you work with my existing landscape?

Absolutely! We specialize in enhancing existing gardens and working around established features.

What's included in maintenance services?

Our maintenance includes pruning, weeding, fertilizing, pest control, and seasonal plant care.
